Are you ready for the sweet life?

La Bella Vista at La Dolce Vita

Enjoy 'the sweet life' at Spring, on Bequia. Set in a tropical garden and kept cool by gentle ocean breezes, La Bella Vista is a private, spacious, one-bedroom apartment with tropical décor featuring local artwork that encompasses the entire lower floor of the villa. The hillside location is a 5-minute walk to the beach and offers spectacular views of Spring Bay and the Atlantic Ocean

The long gallery and private dining terrace provide sweeping views of Spring Bay and the islands of Battowia and Balliceaux as shown in the photo of the view from La Bella Vista. Sit on the deck, swim in the pool, enjoy the breezes and watch the palm trees sway. It is so easy to slide into island time here.

La Dolce Vita is a few minutes walk from Spring beach, about 10 minutes walk to Industry beach, and about 30 minutes walk into Port Elizabeth. No matter where you walk you are surrounded by beauty and lush foliage. 'La Dolce Vita' is just over a mile from Port Elizabeth where its shops, boutiques, restaurants, banks, etc. are located. Rental cars are available from several sources and there are plenty of taxis available.

The people of Bequia are what make Bequia so special, we think. Bequians, though generally shy and reserved, love to meet new people and get to know visitors. Come to Bequia for a visit and when you return people will remember you and welcome you back.
